Why choose us

A historic palazzo,
a cuisine of excellence

Organising a special event in Amantea means choosing the right place. L'800 Locanda a Palazzo offers a unique setting: the rooms of a nineteenth-century palazzo in the heart of the old town, a private garden (in spring and summer), and a Calabrian fish cuisine that never disappoints.

Every event is tailor-made. No pre-packaged formulas: tell us about the occasion, and we'll build the experience around it.

  • Tailored menus based on the occasion and preferences
  • Dedicated spaces for groups up to 70 guests
  • Fresh fish from the Tyrrhenian, premium Calabrian ingredients
  • Historic garden available for outdoor events (spring/summer)
  • Support throughout the event planning
Weddings

Your day, in our garden

Not a banquet hall: a nineteenth-century palazzo with an intimate garden, for weddings of up to 70 guests. Choosing L'800 means choosing intimacy — a reception where every table is close to the couple, a menu built together with the chef around the day's catch and the season, and the lantern-lit garden framing the evening.

The garden is available from May to September; the palazzo's interior rooms host receptions all year round. Every wedding includes a personalised tasting menu, refined through a dedicated menu trial with the couple.

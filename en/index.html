Our story

A nineteenth-century palazzo.
A garden in the heart
of Amantea.

L'800 sits in a historic palazzo in Amantea's old town, on the Calabrian Tyrrhenian coast. The rooms still hold the austere charm of the nineteenth century — high ceilings, exposed stone, low lighting.

But the garden is the real surprise: a green corner in the heart of the city, with soft lighting, the scent of jasmine, and the close-knit atmosphere of those who know that eating well isn't just about food. You don't need to leave Amantea to find somewhere special — it was already here.

People don't come here just to eat.
They come here to feel good.

The cuisine

Every morning the fisherman
brings us what
the sea has decided.

Our cuisine is born from the fresh catch of the Tyrrhenian Sea, interpreted with respect for the Calabrian tradition and a touch of contemporary refinement. From raw shellfish with Calabrian bergamot, to handmade pasta, every dish tells the story of a place.

Our wine cellar

A definite choice.
Wine as
the territory itself.

Our wine list is a deliberate editorial choice: almost exclusively Calabrian labels, many of them from small artisan wineries with very limited distribution. Wineries you would not find in an ordinary wine shop, but that have something authentic and extraordinary to tell.

We believe wine should tell the same story as the food on your plate. That is why every Calabrian appellation — from Cirò to Savuto, from Lamezia to Bivongi — has a place on our list, alongside a small selection of out-of-region labels chosen with the same care.
